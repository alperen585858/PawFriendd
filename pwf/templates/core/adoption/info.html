Frequently Asked Questions

You must be at least 18 years old, have valid identification, and provide proof of residence. We also conduct a brief interview to ensure the pet will be going to a suitable home.

The process typically takes 1-3 days, depending on the completion of necessary paperwork and home visits if required.

We offer a 2-week adjustment period. If things don't work out, you can return the pet and we'll help you find a better match.
